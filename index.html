Cat "Bars"

Bars is the true king of his domain. With his luxurious fur, reminiscent of a snow leopard, and a piercing gaze that can instantly stop anyone who dares to enter his territory, he commands respect. Bars loves spending time on the windowsill, watching the world outside, as if he's guarding his kingdom. His favorite activity is quietly observing everything around him, but when he's in the mood, he can be playful and affectionate, reminding everyone that even kings need some fun.
